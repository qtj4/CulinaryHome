The Importance of Fresh Ingredients

Cooking with fresh ingredients is not only healthier but also makes your meals more flavorful. Let’s explore why freshness is key to culinary success.

Why Fresh Ingredients Matter:

  1. Nutritional Value – Fresh ingredients retain more vitamins and minerals.
  2. Better Taste – Freshly harvested vegetables and fruits offer superior flavor.
  3. Texture – Fresh ingredients provide the right crunch and consistency for different dishes.

Top Ingredients to Always Buy Fresh:

  • Herbs (e.g., basil, cilantro, parsley)
  • Fruits (e.g., berries, citrus)
  • Vegetables (e.g., leafy greens, root vegetables)

Mastering the Basics: Essential Cooking Terms

Understanding basic cooking terminology can make a huge difference in the kitchen. Below, we break down a few essential terms that every home chef should know.

Cooking Terms You Should Know:

Sauté
Cooking food quickly in a small amount of oil or butter over high heat.
Blanch
Boiling food briefly before plunging it into ice water to stop the cooking process.
Braise
Cooking food slowly in a small amount of liquid after browning it.

Key Techniques to Practice:

  1. Sautéing
  2. Simmering
  3. Roasting
